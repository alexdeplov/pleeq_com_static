• Compass Arrow Doesn't Move, What to Do?

    Please check the next settings. It should be ON:
    iOS Settings → Privacy → Location services → System Services → Compass Calibration.

  • Compass Arrow Still Doesn't Move?

    If you have an iPhone case with magnets inside, please remove it and try again.

  • Park Car Function Shows Wrong Distance, What to Do?

    Please check these settings. They should be ON:
    iOS Settings → Privacy → Location services → System Services:

    · Cell Network Search
    · Compass Calibration
    · Location-Based Alerts
    · Motion Calibration & Distance
    · Setting Time Zone
    · Wi-Fi Networking
    · Significant Locations

  • Is it Necessary to Enable Wi-Fi Networking?

    Yes. The GPS in an iPhone is an "Assisted GPS", meaning that it will use known locations of Wi-Fi transmitters to locate the phone if the phone happens to be receiving a Wi-Fi signal that it "knows" the location of. Notice that this does not mean that the Wi-Fi transmitter has to be yours. Your iPhone could be picking up a Wi-Fi station that has been moved from its "known" location.

  • Is it Possible to Find a Car or a Location Inside a Building?

    No. Currently, GPS technology inside your iPhone can't pinpoint your location if you are inside a building. Best results are obtained when the app is used outdoors.
