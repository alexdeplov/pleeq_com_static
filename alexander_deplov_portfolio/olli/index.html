Interface for Olli by Tinrocket

Best of the App Store 2013

Project Summary

Olli by Tinrocket invites you to turn the ordinary into the extraordinary. A groundbreaking photo and video app designed to transform everyday moments into hand-drawn art and animations with live preview. We've built an experience that lets people express creativity by capturing videos or photos and turning them into art on the fly.

My Role

Crafting the app interface from scratch, in close collaboration with developers to intricately design and fine-tune video experiences.

The team

Me as a solo designer, with guidance from a CEO, two developers.
