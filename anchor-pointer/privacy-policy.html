Privacy Policy for Anchor Pointer

Effective Date: Nov 5, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Pleeq Software ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information related to the Anchor Pointer iOS app. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring that no personal data is collected, tracked, or shared through the use of this app.

Data Collection and Usage

We use Firebase to gather anonymous data on app usage to help us understand and improve the app’s performance. This information is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users or their activity. Privacy and Security in Firebase

We do not collect any personal information, such as your name, email, or exact location.

The data collected by Firebase may include: - Device information (e.g., IP address, operating system, browser type) - General app usage patterns, such as feature interactions

We use this information strictly to optimize the app’s performance, conduct research, and enhance user experience.

Push Notifications

If enabled, we may send you push notifications. You can manage these notifications through your device settings.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

While Anchor Pointer does not use traditional cookies, Firebase may use similar tracking technologies within the app to help analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality. These technologies do not collect personal information and are only used in aggregated form to enhance the app experience.

Data Retention

Firebase retains anonymized data for a limited period according to its own data retention policies, after which it is automatically deleted. Pleeq Software does not control or extend the retention period for Firebase data and only accesses anonymized, aggregated information to improve app performance.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell any personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose anonymous, aggregated information:

  • - To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights
  • - In response to requests from government authorities
  • - In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, where notice will be provided on our website

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data collected through the app. However, please note that no method of digital transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of any information transmitted or stored by the app.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices. Material updates will be posted within the app or on our website, with the effective date clearly stated. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us.
