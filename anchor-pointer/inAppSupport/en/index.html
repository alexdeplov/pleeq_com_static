  • Park Car Shows Wrong Distance? Here’s What to Do

    Check these settings and ensure they are ON: iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → System Services:

    · Compass Calibration
    · Location-Based Alerts
    · Location-Based Suggestions
    · Mobile Network Search
    · Motion Calibration & Distance
    · Networking & Wireless
    · Setting Time Zone

  • Is It Necessary to Enable Networking & Wireless?

    Yes. The GPS in an iPhone is "Assisted GPS," meaning it will use known locations of Wi-Fi transmitters to locate the phone if it detects a Wi-Fi signal from a known location. This doesn’t require the Wi-Fi transmitter to be yours; it could be from any nearby Wi-Fi station.

  • Is It Possible to Find a Car or Location Inside a Building?

    No. Currently, GPS technology in your iPhone can’t pinpoint your location inside buildings. For best results, use the app outdoors.

  • Enabled All Settings But Location Is Still Wrong?

    In certain situations—such as densely populated urban areas, indoor spaces with limited connectivity, use of iPhone cases with magnets, or while in a car—the compass accuracy may be affected by magnetic interference. We recommend using the app outdoors, without magnetic covers, and not inside the car.
