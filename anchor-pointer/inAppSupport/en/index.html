  • Compass Not Moving?

    If you have an iPhone case with magnets, please remove it and try again.

  • Wrong Car Distance?

    Check these settings and ensure they are ON: iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → System Services:

    · Compass Calibration: On
    · Location-Based Alerts: On
    · Location-Based Suggestions: On
    · Mobile Network Search: On
    · Motion Calibration & Distance: On
    · Networking & Wireless: On

  • Why Enable Networking?

    The GPS in an iPhone is "Assisted GPS," meaning it will use known locations of Wi-Fi transmitters to locate the phone if it detects a Wi-Fi signal from a known location. According to Apple this doesn’t require the Wi-Fi transmitter to be yours; it could be from any nearby Wi-Fi station.

  • Indoor Location Possible?

    No. Currently, GPS technology in your iPhone can’t pinpoint your location inside buildings. For best results, use the app outdoors.

  • Everything Fails?

    In certain situations—such as densely populated urban areas, indoor spaces with limited connectivity, use of iPhone cases with magnets, or while in a car—the compass accuracy may be affected by magnetic interference. We recommend using the app outdoors, without magnetic covers, and not inside the car.
